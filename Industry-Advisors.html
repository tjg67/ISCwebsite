The Industry Advisory Group

The industry advisory group members are prominent scientists and leaders that were selected to represent key segments and application areas of critical importance to the AMPED consortium objectives. The advisory group provides the faculty leadership team with insights about emerging needs and trends within relevant industries and across the various agencies.
